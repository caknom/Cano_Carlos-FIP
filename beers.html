Our Beers

Amber Lager

Is a celebration of rich
Caramel flavors balanced by a smooth hop finish. This medium-bodied lager has a deep amber color that promises a malt-forward taste and a clean finish. Crafted with a blend of specialty malts, it offers a toasted complexity that is both approachable and satisfying. Perfect for those who appreciate a beer with depth but without the heaviness of a darker brew.

Light Lager

Refreshment and drinkability
This crisp and clean lager is made with the finest ingredients, resulting in a light golden brew with a delicate balance of malt and hops. Its subtle flavors and low bitterness make it an excellent choice for those seeking a lighter beer option that doesn't compromise on taste.

Dark Lager

Rich and Complex
Offers a complex flavor profile with a surprisingly smooth finish. This full-bodied lager is brewed with a variety of dark roasted malts, imparting a deep ebony color and flavors of chocolate and coffee. Despite its robust appearance, it remains smooth and easy to drink, with just the right amount of hop bitterness to balance the malt sweetness.
