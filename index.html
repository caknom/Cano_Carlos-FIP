Billy Beer: The Original Brew

Billy Beer, the iconic brand from 1977, is making a comeback! This American classic, endorsed by Billy Carter, brother of President Jimmy Carter, is your chance to taste a piece of history.

Brewed with care and Billy's signature approval, Billy Beer is a refreshing and satisfying lager. Don't miss this chance to be a part of Billy Beer's legacy! Try it today and experience the taste of history!

Our Beers

AMBER LAGER

Brewed with a touch of caramel malt, Billy Beer Amber Lager boasts a rich amber hue and a smooth, malty flavor with hints of toasted bread and toffee. This medium-bodied lager is perfect for those who appreciate a balanced and flavorful beer.

LIGHT LAGER

Billy Beer Light Lager is a golden delight, brewed for pure refreshment. Its light body and clean finish make it the ideal companion for a hot day or a casual gathering. Enjoy the subtle sweetness and crispness of this easy-drinking lager.

DARK LAGER

Billy Beer Dark Lager offers a unique twist on the classic recipe. Deep amber in color, it boasts a rich, roasted flavor profile with hints of chocolate and coffee. This full-bodied lager is perfect for those who appreciate a bolder beer experience.
